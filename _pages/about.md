Hi I'm Anderson, a 5th year statistics PhD student at the University of South Carolina where I work under the supervision of Dr. Yen-Yi Ho.

My main research focus is on developing methods for analyzing complex dependencies in single-cell and spatial transcriptomics data. This work incorporates elements of computational biology, high-dimensional data, hierarchical modeling, and flexible joint dependence modeling. Additionally, I am working on interdisciplinary research projects in the fields of insurance/risk, biology, and computer science.
