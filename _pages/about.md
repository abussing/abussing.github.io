I am a 4th year PhD student at the University of South Carolina, where I work under Professor Yen-Yi Ho on methods for analyzing differential co-expression in single-cell RNAseq data. 

I am interested in developing and implementing models to investigate how characteristics such as spatial location, cell type, and methylation level impact gene-gene correlation within cells. Furthermore, I wish to explore how different cell types interact within a tissue, using ligands and receptors to regulate cellular processes.  

Before attending graduate school, I was in the operations management leadership program at General Electric, where I did rotations as a lead buyer, manufacturing engineer, and shop operations supervisor.
