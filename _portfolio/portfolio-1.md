---
title: "scDECO"
excerpt: "R package implementing two different Bayesian models for estimating differential coexpression"
collection: portfolio
---

Provides implementations for the bivariate Gaussian copula model from [https://doi.org/10.1111/biom.13701](https://doi.org/10.1111/biom.13701), as well as the bivariate Poisson-Gamma model from [https://doi.org/10.1111/biom.13457](https://doi.org/10.1111/biom.13457).

[GitHub Repository](https://github.com/YenYiHo-Lab/scDECO)

[CRAN Package](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/scDECO/index.html)
