Hi I'm Anderson, a 5th year statistics PhD student at the University of South Carolina where I work under the supervision of Dr. Yen-Yi Ho.

My main research focus is on developing methods for analyzing complex dependencies in single-cell and spatial transcriptomics data. This research incorporates elements of computational biology, high-dimensional data, hierarchical modeling, and flexible joint dependence modeling. Additionally, I work on various interdisciplinary projects with collaborators from biology, insurance, and computer science.
