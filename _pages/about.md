Hi I'm Anderson, a 5th year statistics PhD student at the University of South Carolina working under the supervision of Dr. Yen-Yi Ho.

My research focuses on analyzing gene-gene dependencies in single-cell and spatial transcriptomics data. Broadly, I am interested in computational biology, high-dimensional data, and joint dependence modeling, with an emphasis on creating create useful open-source software tools. Beyond my work in genomics, I also engage in interdisciplinary research with collaborators from biology, insurance, and computer science.
