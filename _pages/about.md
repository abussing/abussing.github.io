Hi I'm Anderson, a 5th year statistics PhD student at the University of South Carolina working under the supervision of Dr. Yen-Yi Ho.

My research focuses on analyzing complex dependencies in single-cell and spatial transcriptomics data. This research lies at the intersection of computational biology, high-dimensional data, and joint dependence modeling, and my goal is to create useful open-source software tools for interrogating how gene-gene dependence structures drive transcriptional programs across cell contexts. Beyond my work in transcriptomics, I also engage in interdisciplinary research with collaborators from biology, insurance, and computer science.
