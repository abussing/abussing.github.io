Hi I'm Anderson, a 5th year statistics PhD student at the University of South Carolina working under the supervision of Dr. Yen-Yi Ho.

My research focuses on developing methods for the analysis of complex dependencies in single-cell and spatial transcriptomics data. This research incorporates elements of computational biology, high-dimensional data, hierarchical modeling, and flexible joint dependence modeling. I also enjoy engaging in interdisciplinary research with collaborators from biology, insurance, and computer science.
