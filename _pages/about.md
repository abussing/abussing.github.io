Hi I'm Anderson, a 5th year statistics PhD student at the University of South Carolina where I work under the supervision of Dr. Yen-Yi Ho.

My research deals with analyzing complex dependencies in single-cell and spatial transcriptomics data. This work incorporates elements of computational biology, high-dimensional data, hierarchical modeling, and flexible joint dependence modeling. I also enjoy cross-disciplinary collaboration and developing open-source statistical software tools.
